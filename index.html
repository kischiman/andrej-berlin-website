Building is solved. Adoption isn't.

Teams ship faster and add more: more features, more surface, more impressive demos. But the graveyard from past cycles is full of impressive demos. Most startups burn their runway on bloat the market never asked for. Around 70% of shutdowns trace back to the same two causes: no real market need, and running out of cash trying to find one.

Adoption comes from experiences that capture attention, hold interest, and stay memorable for years.
